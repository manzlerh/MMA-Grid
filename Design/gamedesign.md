5. Game Design Details

Initial Version only two games: 

UFC Grid (Tic-Tac-Toe style)

3x3 grid where rows and columns are UFC attributes
Example axes: Weight Class × Nationality, Gym × Finish Type, Era × Title Status
Player types a fighter name → it autocompletes → validates against both the row and column attribute
Scoring: fewer guesses = higher score; rarity bonus (if a fighter fits only one cell)

UFC Connections

16 fighters grouped into 4 hidden categories of 4
Categories like: "All fought Conor McGregor", "American Top Team fighters", "Former Lightweight Champions", "Wrestlers who finished fights by KO"
Color-coded difficulty (yellow → purple)
5 wrong guesses allowed before game over
